RideWise is an intelligent bike-sharing demand prediction platform that uses machine learning to forecast bike rental demand. Whether you're managing a bike-sharing service or planning your daily commute, RideWise helps you make data-driven decisions by predicting hourly and daily bike demand based on weather conditions, time of day, and day type.

What RideWise Does:

  • Hourly Predictions: Forecast bike demand for any specific hour of the day
  • Daily Predictions: Estimate total daily bike rentals with hourly breakdown
  • Weather Integration: Considers temperature, humidity, windspeed, and weather conditions
  • Smart Analysis: Factors in weekends, holidays, and working days

Use Cases:

  • Bike-Sharing Operators: Optimize bike distribution and inventory management
  • City Planners: Understand demand patterns for better infrastructure planning
  • Commuters: Plan ahead by knowing when bikes will be available
  • Operations Teams: Schedule maintenance and rebalancing activities
